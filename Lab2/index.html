Our Story

GreenSense was born from a simple frustration: watching beautiful plants wither despite our best intentions. Founded in 2023 by a team of plant lovers and tech enthusiasts, we set out to bridge the gap between nature and technology.

Our mission is to make plant parenting accessible to everyone. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or someone who's killed every plant they've owned, our smart pots give you the insights you need to help your green friends flourish.
